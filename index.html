Darts League

The premier darts league for competitive players. Join a community of passionate players and take your game to the next level.

Join WhatsApp Community

🏆 League Format

Standard league format: First to win 5 legs wins the match. If the score is 4-4, the match is a draw. Compete in a structured league with players at your skill level and climb the rankings.

🎯 CAM Scoring

Computer Aided Marking - scores are automatically calculated. Play online using the DartCounter app. All matches are tracked and verified through the platform.

🪙 Tokens & Rewards

Earn Elite Tokens for winning league games (+100 for a win). Tokens can be used for tournament entries and other rewards throughout the season.

📅 Season Structure

Each season runs for a set period with regular fixtures. Stay on top of your schedule and make sure to play your matches before the deadline.

⚖️ Code of Conduct

Zero tolerance for cheating or toxic behavior. Breaking rules results in a one-time final warning followed by an immediate season ban for any subsequent offense.

📱 Platform

All matches are played using the DartCounter app. Make sure you have it installed and your account set up before the season starts.
