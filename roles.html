Job / Role Applications

📹 Streamer / Content Creator

Stream league matches and create content for the Elite Arrows community. Help grow our presence on Twitch, YouTube, and other platforms.

  • Reliable streaming setup (OBS, decent PC, stable internet)
  • Basic video editing skills for highlights and recaps
  • Comfortable commentating live matches
  • Active on Twitch, YouTube, or TikTok
  • Available to stream at least 2-3 sessions per week
Streaming Content Creation Video Editing Commentary
👮 Moderator / Admin

Help moderate the WhatsApp community and league chat. Enforce the code of conduct, handle disputes fairly, and keep the community running smoothly.

  • Active in the Elite Arrows community and familiar with the rules
  • Strong communication and conflict resolution skills
  • Fair and unbiased decision-making
  • Available to monitor chats for a few hours daily
  • Previous moderation experience is a plus
Community Management Conflict Resolution Leadership
📝 Social Media Manager

Manage the Elite Arrows social media presence across platforms. Create engaging posts, interact with the community, and help grow our follower base.

  • Experience managing social media accounts (Instagram, Twitter/X, TikTok)
  • Ability to create engaging graphics and posts (Canva, Photoshop, etc.)
  • Knowledge of social media trends and growth strategies
  • Post at least 3-4 times per week consistently
  • Previous social media management experience preferred
Social Media Content Planning Graphic Design Marketing
🤝 Sponsorship Coordinator

Reach out to potential sponsors, manage partnerships, and secure funding or prizes for the league and its tournaments.

  • Strong communication and negotiation skills
  • Experience reaching out to businesses or brands
  • Ability to create sponsorship proposals and pitch decks
  • Organised with a professional approach
  • Previous sponsorship or sales experience preferred
Sponsorship Negotiation Business Development
🏆 Tournament Organiser

Plan, organise, and run league tournaments. Create brackets, schedule matches, and ensure tournaments run smoothly from start to finish.

  • Experience organising brackets and tournament schedules
  • Strong organisational and time management skills
  • Familiar with common tournament formats (round-robin, knockout, etc.)
  • Available to host and oversee tournament sessions
  • Previous tournament organisation experience preferred
Tournaments Planning Brackets Scheduling
🎪 Event Coordinator

Organise online events including future cups, charity events, and special community tournaments. Handle scheduling, coordination, and ensure everything runs smoothly.

  • Experience planning and running online events
  • Strong communication and coordination skills
  • Ability to manage multiple tasks and deadlines
  • Creative mindset for coming up with event ideas
  • Available to host events during evenings or weekends
Event Planning Logistics Coordination
🔍 Recruiter

Find and recruit new players to join the Elite Arrows league. Promote the league across darts communities and help grow our player base.

  • Active in darts communities (Discord, Reddit, Facebook groups, etc.)
  • Strong interpersonal and outreach skills
  • Familiar with the league format and rules to answer questions
  • Friendly and welcoming approach to newcomers
  • Previous recruitment or community outreach experience is a plus
Recruitment Outreach Community Building
🎙️ Interview Process

Once your role application has been approved, you will be contacted via email to arrange a short interview. Interviews are held via messages or voice call and typically last 5-10 minutes. This is a chance for us to get to know you better and discuss the role in more detail.
